## Contextual AI assistant

Problem: New resource joins team → spends 2-3 weeks understanding "why we built it this way"

GenAI Solution:

Auto-indexed knowledge graph that connects:

Design decisions (Docx/email/meetings) → Implementation (code) → Business context
"Why did we choose Threshold #1 over Threshold #2?" → Historical decisions → Current tradeoffs
Links between PRs, design docs, business decisions, and outcomes


Contextual AI assistant that explains: "This module exists because of XYZ business requirement from 2022, which changed when we pivoted to bussiness"
Reduces onboarding from 3 weeks to 3 days -- and also in a way would help us follow up on our audit realted things. 
Audit may not make it to the list of SDLC -- but the benifit is a side product.

## Domain & Business Logic Extraction (Post Facto SDLC) 

Problem: Business logic is scattered across code, docs, spreadsheets, tribal knowledge. New feature requests need context on existing business rules.

GenAI Solution:

Business Rule Mining: Scans code + docs + requirements → extracts:

"RDFI Name match can only use levenshtien with thresold of 90 except for FA on the account name is being matched"
"Vietnam/ South Africa rules check the demographies on thorugh certain mechanism"
All the hidden business logic buried in code

Rule Engine Generation: Creates testable, queryable rules
Impact Analysis: When new feature comes in, shows which business rules it affects
Audit Compliance Mapping: Links business rules to Audit requirements

Decision Documentation & Rationale Capture
Problem: Decisions made in sync calls → 6 months later no one knows WHY → wrong assumption based decision reversed
GenAI Solution:

Meeting Intelligence: Auto-transcribes/summarizes engineering decisions from:

Design sync meetings
Brainstorming sessions
Post-mortems
Architecture discussions

Generates decision records with:

The Decision: What we're doing
Alternatives Considered: What we rejected and why
Tradeoffs: What we're giving up
Assumptions: What must remain true for this to be right
Review Date: When to re-evaluate if assumptions change

Assumption Monitoring: Flags when captured assumptions become invalid
